Benefits:

  • Increases creativity (AI-assisted writing, design, and music)
  • Enhances productivity (automating repetitive tasks)
  • Personalization (AI-generated ads, chatbots)
  • Accessibility (text-to-speech for visually impaired users)

Limitations:

  • Misinformation & Deepfakes – AI can create fake but realistic content.
  • Bias in AI – AI models reflect biases in their training data.
  • Plagiarism & Copyright Issues – AI-generated content raises questions about ownership.
  • Job Displacement – Automation may replace certain jobs.
